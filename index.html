The game is played with alternating players. For each turn, the active player rolls the die and chooses whether or not to 'hold,' thereby adding their current roll(s) to their total score. If a one is rolled, the active player's current score goes to zero and their turn ends. The first player to reach the total score of 100 wins the game.
